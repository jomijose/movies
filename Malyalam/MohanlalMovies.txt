	1.Manjil Virinja Pookkal
        Manjil Virinja Pookkal (English: Flowers That Bloomed in the Mist) is a 1980 Indian Malayalam musical romance film produced by Navodaya Appachan, written and directed by Fazil. It stars Shankar, Poornima Jayaram, and Mohanlal. The original soundtrack was composed by Jerry Amaldev and cinematography was done by Ashok Kumar.
    2.Sanchari
        Sanchari is a 1981 Indian Malayalam film, directed and produced by Boban Kunchacko. The film stars Prem Nazir, Jayan, Mohanlal and Adoor Bhasi in lead roles. The film had musical score by K. J. Yesudas.[1][2][3]
